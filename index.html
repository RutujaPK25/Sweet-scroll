SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple choclate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

EQUIPMENT

  • Cake tin or muffin tray
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer
  • Cooling rack
  • Fork
  • Weighing scales
  • Smaller bowls, to hold ingredients

INGREDIENTS

  • 65 gram of coca
  • 2 eggs
  • 250 gram butter
  • 125 mls of milk
  • 220 grams of caster sugar
  • Topping of your choice

PREPARATION

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Grease your cake tin or muffin tray
  3. Sift your flour and cocoa into the mixing bowl
  4. Beat eggs and add it to the mixing bowl
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients to the bowl
  6. Pour the mix into the cake into the cake tin or muffin tray

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Bake cake for 35-40 minutes
  2. For cup cakes bake for 12-15 minutes
  3. Put a cake tester or toothpick into the center of the cake, if it comes out clean, your cake is done.
  4. Place cake or muffins on the cooling tray and wait to cool.

DECORATIONS

Once the cakeor cupcake have cooled, decorate them. You can choose whatever toppings or decoration you like!

Pasta

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple choclate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

INGREDIENTS

  1. Penne Pasta or any pasta
  2. Salt, Water, Oil, Veggies
  3. Butter
  4. Chopped Garlic
  5. Maida(all purpose flavour)
  6. Milk
  7. Oregano

PREPARATION

  1. Boil raw pasta
  2. Transfer cooked pasta to a large colander and drain excess water
  3. Heat the oil and add chopped veggies and stir it
  4. Heat butter in the same pan and add chopped garlic
  5. Add maida and ther add milk
  6. Mix well. Then add chopped veggies to it.

DECORATIONS

Once the pasta is ready you can add oregano and chilly flex according to the test.
